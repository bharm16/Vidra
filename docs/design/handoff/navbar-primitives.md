# The navbar

Persistent top bar. Present over the workspace and the supporting pages.

## Contains

- **Wordmark** — goes to the workspace root.
- **Session label** (workspace only) — shows the current session's title, or an untitled
  placeholder. Display only.
- **Library** — goes to past work: prior sessions and kept clips.
- **Account region**
  - Signed in — opens the account menu: open the account page, sign out.
  - Signed out — opens the auth dialog.
- **Call to action** (supporting pages only) — signed in: open the workspace; signed out:
  sign in.
