# The rail

Persistent chrome of the app: a slim left rail, collapsible between labeled and icon-only.
Present over the workspace; supporting pages carry the same identity in their own header.

## Contains

- **Wordmark** — goes to the workspace root.
- **Library** — goes to past work: prior sessions and kept clips.
- **Collapse toggle** — switches the rail between labeled and icon-only.
- **Account region** (pinned at the bottom)
  - Signed in — opens the account menu: open the account page, sign out.
  - Signed out — opens the auth dialog.
- **Call to action** (supporting pages only) — signed in: open the workspace; signed out:
  sign in.

The session title sits at the top of the content area, above the space — display only.
